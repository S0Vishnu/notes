
## **1. Sell Digital Products on Etsy or Gumroad**

Your design + story skills = high-value digital items.

You can create:

- Children’s storybooks (AI illustrated)
- Short moral stories / bedtime stories
- Graphic templates (flyers, posters, thumbnails)
- Social media templates
- Journals, planners, workbooks
- Coloring books with AI art
- Story-based printable calendars

**Why it's perfect for you:**  
You design the visuals and create stories; AI helps with polishing, formatting, and variety.

> Build once → sell forever.

## **2. Publish Storybooks on Amazon KDP**

If you can create stories, you can turn them into:

- Kids’ picture books    
- Short story collections    
- Motivational stories    
- Fantasy stories    
- Comics (AI illustrated)    

**Cost:** Free  
**Passive income:** Royalties from every sale

Workflow:

1. Write story (or outline)    
2. Use AI to refine & expand    
3. Design illustrations    
4. Format the book    
5. Publish on Amazon KDP    

This can genuinely earn passive income once the book is up.
